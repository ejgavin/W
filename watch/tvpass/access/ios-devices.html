How to Set Up IPTVX for TVPass on Apple TV

Looking to watch TVPass on your Apple TV? IPTVX makes it easy to stream live channels with an M3U playlist. Follow this quick setup guide to start watching your favorite content instantly.

Step 1: Download IPTVX from the App Store

Go to the Apple Store on your Apple TV and search for IPTVX. Click Download and install the app.

Step 2: Open IPTVX & Create a Playlist

Launch IPTVX and navigate to the menu. Tap on New Playlist to start adding your channels.

Step 3: Enter M3U URL & Configure EPG

Select M3U playlist via URL and enter the details:

  • Playlist Name
  • M3U Playlist URL
  • EPG URL (for TV guide functionality)
  • Set Reload Frequency to Daily for automatic updates.

Step 4: Save & Access Your Playlist

After entering the details, tap Save and select your newly created playlist.

Step 5: Browse & Watch Live TV

Navigate the TV Guide and start watching your favorite live channels.

Step 6: Organize Your Channels

Filter and categorize channels based on playlists, sports, and other preferences.

Step 7: Enjoy High-Quality Streaming

Now you're all set! Enjoy live TV with seamless streaming on your Apple TV.
